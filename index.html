About Us

Ezulwini Stay

Ezulwini Stay is a Standerton (Mpumalanga) based company that provides fully furnished self-catering flats and rooms, all equipped with TV and optional DSTV bouquet.

We offer an upmarket home-like experience where our guests are comfortable and enjoy their stay at the Lodge, ensuring guests of superior accommodation and service. Providing an ideal location that creates unforgettable leisure and business experience, by offering guests a pleasant Experience at all times.
